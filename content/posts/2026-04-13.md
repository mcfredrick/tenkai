## Research Worth Reading
- [OpenKedge: Governing Agentic Mutation with Execution-Bound Safety and Evidence Chains](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08601) — Proposes OpenKedge to control AI agent mutations as a governed process, using execution-bound safety and evidence chains to manage probabilistic risks.
- [From Business Events to Auditable Decisions: Ontology-Governed Graph Simulation for Enterprise AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08603) — Grounds LLM-based agent decisions in business event simulation to produce auditable outcomes with traceable decision trails.
- [Sustained Impact of Agentic Personalisation in Marketing: A Longitudinal Case Study](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08621) — Examines how long agentic personalization in marketing CRM holds up and why human-in-the-loop oversight still matters.
- [RAMP: Hybrid DRL for Online Learning of Numeric Action Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08685) — Introduces a hybrid DRL approach to learn numeric action models from observations, skipping the need for offline expert traces.
- [SPPO: Sequence-Level PPO for Long-Horizon Reasoning Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08865) — Addresses temporal credit instability in long-horizon reasoning with sequence-level PPO for LLMs using verifiable rewards.
- [StaRPO: Stability-Augmented Reinforcement Policy Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08905) — Augments RL with stability considerations to capture the internal logical structure of reasoning, not just final-answer correctness.
